Sands of Arrakis

Survive the harsh desert planet. Manage your thirst, harvest the precious Spice, and beware of the giant Sandworms that sense rhythmic vibrations in the sand.

Controls:
[W, A, S, D] - Move
[SPACE] - Jump
[MOUSE] - Look around
[C] - Toggle Creative Mode
[M] - Toggle Admin Menu (in Creative Mode)

Survival Tips:
- Blue Oasis regions contain water. Walk through them to drink.
- Orange Crystals are Spice. They restore health and grant score.
- Rocky Plateaus mask your footsteps from Sandworms.
- Keep moving during Sandstorms, and watch out for flying spice!
